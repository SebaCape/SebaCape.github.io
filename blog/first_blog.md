## Welcome.
To my new portfolio website. I ultimately decided on revamping it, as my old one was made in my freshman year following a tutorial beat by beat, and I figured that at this point, it was something that I had outgrown to an extent.

Another reason for this migration is because of what lies in front of your eyes right now: a blog page. Beyond the new stylish overhaul, this website is also functionally better as with a blog I can now share my insights on industry, new technologies, and provide deep dives into projects that I create/am working on with an overview of what informs my design choices. I think of a blog in a similar way to a public journal, where it allows one to better practice metacognition, which is a great skill to have when engineering large components of software.

That is about it for this entry, if you are new here; welcome. I hope you enjoy yourself in exploring around who I am and what I have to offer both professionally, and to some degree, personally.
